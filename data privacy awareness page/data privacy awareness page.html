Data Privacy Awareness

Data privacy is the protection of personal information on the internet. In today's digital world, people share large amounts of personal data online through social media, websites, and apps. Understanding privacy helps protect users from cyber threats and hackers.

What is Data Privacy?

Personal Control

Data privacy allows individuals to control how their personal information is collected, used, and shared.

Online Data

Every time we browse websites or use apps, data is generated and stored by companies.

Security

Privacy protection ensures sensitive data such as passwords and banking details remain secure.

Hackers and Cyber Attacks

Hackers are individuals who attempt to gain unauthorized access to computer systems and personal information. They often use malware, phishing emails, and fake websites to trick users.

Cyber attacks can lead to identity theft, financial fraud, and privacy violations. Learning about these threats helps people stay alert and protect their personal information.

Common Privacy Risks

Phishing

Fake emails and websites designed to steal login details and financial information.

Malware

Malicious software that steals personal data or damages systems.

Data Breaches

Large cyber attacks where hackers steal millions of user records.

Location Tracking

Apps and websites track user locations through GPS and IP addresses.

How to Protect Your Data

  • Use strong passwords for all online accounts.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links.
  • Use antivirus and firewall protection.
  • Update software regularly.
  • Use secure HTTPS websites.
